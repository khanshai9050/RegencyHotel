ABOUT US

Budget Luxury Hotel In The Mid Of City

Hotel Regency is the best budget hotel in Varanasi in the heart of the city, hotel features spacious 40 Rooms for luxurious accommodation offering complimentary Breakfasts, Wi-Fi Connectivity, Restaurant, Conference Rooms, Banquet halls, all under one roof. “Peacock, Robin & Board Room” two banquet Halls and a board room which can host 15 to 400 people at a moment with all arrangements of corporate meet, conferences, marriage party, personal functions & parties.

The hotel is located in the business & religious hub of the city, Near Varanasi Railway Station. Experience splendid accommodation in Hotel Regency, Varanasi with a wide array of facilities catering to the needs of elite business, corporate and pilgrim travellers.
